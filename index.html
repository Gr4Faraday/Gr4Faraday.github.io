Fluid Mechanics

Fluid mechanics is a discipline of physics that studies the mechanics of fluids and the forces acting on them.
Mechanical, aerospace, civil, chemical, and biological engineering, geophysics, oceanography, meteorology, astrophysics, and biology are just a few of the
fields where it might be used .It is the study of how fluids respond to forces applied to them.Fluid Mechanics is also the discipline of science that examines
the behavior of fluids in motion and at rest. Whether the fluid is at rest or motion, it is subjected to diverse forces and different environmental conditions
and it responds in these conditions as per its physical qualities. It is concerned with three elements of fluids: statics, kinematics, and dynamics. Surface
tension, fluid statics, flow in enclosed bodies or flow round bodies (solid or otherwise), flow stability, and other issues are addressed in this subject field.
In reality, nearly every move a human takes involves some sort of fluid mechanics problem.

Simulations

FloReal simulations were made to be able to adequately display the scientific concepts. The simulations on this page, as can be observed from the website, are all concerned with fluid mechanics, specifically the principles of Bernoulli, Pascal, Archimedes, as well as density and pressure.

Pascal's Law

When the pressure in a confined fluid rises at any point, it rises equally at all other points in the container.

Archimedes's Principle

When a body is immersed in a fluid, the fluid exerts an upward force equal to the weight of the fluid displaced by the body.

Bernoulli's Principle

When the velocity of the fluid is high, the pressure is low and when the velocity is low, the pressure is high

Who We Are

FloReal is an online-based Fluid Mechanics simulator open to every curious mind. It is facilitated and created by five (5) student-researchers of Rizal National Science High School, in fulfillment to their requirements in Research Capstone. FloReal enables learners, as well as teachers, to immerse themselves with the simulations of Fluid Mechanics by deploying a wide variety of techniques to make learning fun, engaging, and memorable.

Our Vision

We envision a world with equitable access to science learning and resources. With FloReal, learners everywhere can experience the joy of discovery through interactive learning content.
